About

As a "front-end web developer", what do I do?

I have the power to create a fully functioning website out of nothing. I specialise in manipulating the very fabric of the web, its raw materials of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and SVG, the building blocks of the web.

I can plan, design, build, launch and maintain a website myself. I can even build games! I use Unity along with C# to develop my personal project games and one will soon be ready on the App Store!

My art & design background informs ideation to design through to implementation. In 2019 I achieved a certificate from a workshop in Kosovo on digital arts and a mantra to always consider my audience. I will always advocate for the user of my interfaces; accessibility is not optional.
